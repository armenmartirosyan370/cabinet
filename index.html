Who We Are

We are two brothers, Vache and Vanik, who have a deep-rooted love for the art of finish carpentry and refinishing work. Our journey in this industry began under the expert guidance of our father, a second-generation cabinet builder, who instilled in us a strong work ethic and a passion for craftsmanship. Growing up in our father's workshop, our days were filled with the scent of sawdust and the rhythmic hum of woodworking machinery. We remember watching in awe as he transformed raw pieces of wood into intricate cabinets, each a testament to his skill and dedication. As youngsters, our first lessons were in the importance of precision and attention to detail – values that remain at the core of our work today.

Our Proven Process

After years of fine tuning our craft, we believe we have found the optimal refinishing process; increasing quality and minimizing the stress to you. 

Step 1: Consultation and Assessment Our process commences with a detailed consultation. We closely evaluate your existing cabinets, considering factors like their condition, style, and your specific requirements. This step enables us to recommend the optimal finish that will both enhance aesthetics and serve your practical needs seamlessly.

Step 2: Precise Removal and Protection In step two, we meticulously remove all cabinet doors and drawer faces. We take great care in covering your cabinets, walls, countertops, and surrounding areas, minimizing any mess or disruption to your daily routine. Protection is key, and we prioritize your convenience.

Step 3: Refinishing the Frames The heart of our process lies in step three. Here, we embark on sanding and refinishing the cabinet frames. We ensure that every detail is attended to, leaving the frames looking refreshed and ready for the next phase.

Step 4: Artful Refinishing of Doors and Drawer Faces Step four involves transporting the doors and drawer faces to our shop. Here, we undertake a meticulous process of sanding, prepping, and refinishing each piece. Our commitment to excellence is showcased in this phase, as we pour our passion into restoring these elements to their former beauty.

Step 5: Reinstallation and Final Flourish With step five, we bring everything full circle. We carefully re-install the revitalized doors and drawer faces, ensuring a precise fit. Finishing touches are added to guarantee that the result surpasses your expectations. A thorough cleaning ensures that your space is as immaculate as your refurbished cabinets.

Who We Are

We are two brothers, Vache and Vanik, who have a deep-rooted love for the art of finish carpentry and refinishing work. Our journey in this industry began under the expert guidance of our father, a second-generation cabinet builder, who instilled in us a strong work ethic and a passion for craftsmanship. Growing up in our father's workshop, our days were filled with the scent of sawdust and the rhythmic hum of woodworking machinery. We remember watching in awe as he transformed raw pieces of wood into intricate cabinets, each a testament to his skill and dedication. As youngsters, our first lessons were in the importance of precision and attention to detail – values that remain at the core of our work today. Our role in the workshop wasn't just that of apprentices; we were hands-on contributors from a young age. We vividly recall the hours spent meticulously sanding and prepping cabinets for painting, understanding that even the smallest imperfection could compromise the final result. These early experiences laid the foundation for the quality-focused approach we bring to every project we undertake. When fate led us to America, we carried with us the invaluable skills passed down through generations. Inspired by our father's legacy and the desire to channel our passion into something meaningful, we embarked on a new chapter – Cabinet Refinishing Specialist. With years of hands-on experience and an innate understanding of woodwork, we set out to transform dull and dated cabinets into stunning, revitalized pieces that breathe new life into spaces. What truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence and our genuine love for what we do. For us, every cabinet refinishing project is an opportunity to fuse traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics. We approach each piece with the same enthusiasm as we did in our father's workshop, ensuring that every brushstroke and every finish is executed flawlessly. We invite you to experience the legacy of craftsmanship that spans generations.
